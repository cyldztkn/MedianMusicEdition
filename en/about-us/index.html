About Us

Median is an independent music publishing company providing rights management, music selection, new composition creation, and creative services to both local and international creators since 2008. Based in Istanbul with international partners (Median UK in the United Kingdom, Median Musikverlag in Germany, and representative companies in 23 countries), Median represents Turkey’s music creators abroad.
